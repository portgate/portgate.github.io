INTERNATIONAL TERMS

The transportation industry has a unique vocabulary. The global logistics management team must be familiar with these freight terms.

CMR

An international agreement that contains the rights and obligations of parties involved in road transport. “Contrat de Transport International de Marchandises par Route“

AETR

The European Agreement Concerning the Work of Crews of Vehicles Engaged in International Road Transport (AETR) rules are now the same as the EU rules on drivers’ hours.
